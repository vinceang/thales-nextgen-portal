Centered placeholder when a view has nothing to show (no results, empty list, offline).

```jsx
<EmptyState icon="search" title="No results"
  message="Try a different title or browse by genre."
  action={<Button variant="secondary">Browse Movies</Button>} />
```

- Quiet line icon in a hairline ring, a **Montserrat** title (UI face — serif is reserved for media tiles/heroes), a muted guidance line, and at most one action. Flat and centered, one accent.
- Keep copy editorial and short — name the situation, offer one way forward. Don't stack multiple CTAs or add decorative art.
