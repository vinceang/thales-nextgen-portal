News headline row. `lead` renders the large stacked story with a Playfair headline.

```jsx
<NewsItem lead image={src} headline="Trinity Rodman's extra-time stunner…" timestamp="an hour ago" />
<NewsItem image={src} headline="Paris 2024 Olympics medal table" timestamp="6 minutes ago" />
```

- Compact rows use a 96×64 thumbnail + Montserrat 600 headline. Stack in a list with hairline dividers.
